Video Games

During my freetime, I enjoy playing video games. Below is a list of my favorite games as well as a brief description of what each game is about.

Valorant

Valorant is a tactical 5v5 shooter game in which two teams compete to win rounds. The final winner is the first to reach 13 rounds. I enjoy playing this game because of the competitve mode it offers in which I can rank worldwide against other players.

Rocket League

Rocket League is essentially a car soccer game that offers multiple modes but mainly a 2v2 or 3v3 mode. The game works the same as a regular soccer game, in which two teams have a 5 minute time limit to score as many goals as possible.
